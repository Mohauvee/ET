The Lion's World

What Is It like?

Aloysius Italus is a 19 year old gladiator in the time of ancient Rome. There are all kinds of physical features near his home, including mountains, canyons, and valleys. There is also a castle where his rival Magnus Juventus lives, and where he hopes to live after defeating Magnus in combat. The castle is also where gladiator matches often occur.
